A Party Picnic

In 1961 the Iran Party, the main Party of the National Front, organized a 'party picnic' for members near Tehran. Some of the leaders presented lectures about the party's political line and its basic ideology. These are pictures of that event.


Seated from left: Ahmad Zirakzadeh, Asghar Parsa, Allahyar Saleh, Mrs. Saleh.
First one from left standing is Mr. Zolnour, then a student member, who recited the long poem 'Arash-e Kamangir' by heart at the event.
