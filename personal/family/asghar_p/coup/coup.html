US-British Coup of 1953

In the Summer of 1953, US and Britain decided that the government of Dr. Mossadiq should be overthrone. In anticipation, Mossadiq resorted to a referendum to terminate the parliament which was controlled by a majority of anti-Mossadiq members. Asghar Parsa is seen casting his vote in the city of Rezaieh (now Urmiah) just days befor the US-British Coup.

He was arrested in Khoy and transfered to Tehran.
