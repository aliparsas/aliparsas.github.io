A Shipwreck in Miankaleh
Feb. 2005

In Feb. 2005 I visited the shipwreck found in the Caspian coast, near the Miankaleh peninsula, north of Iran. It seems that due to fluctuations in the level of the Caspian Sea in the last decade, the wreck has been exposed from under the coastal sand.

Given the type and the construction, this ship might well have been one of the ships of either Stepan Razin, the cossack who frequented these shores around 1650, or Peter the Great, who invaded Mazandaran in 1722.
