My Trip to
Central America

In 2006 me and Shahrzad made a trip to Central America. The trip was cut short by my father's death back in Tehran. We had to go back and be in the memorial service for him. We planned to go to Mexico, Guatemala, Nicaragua, Costa Rica and Panama. We only made it to Mexico and Guatemala, with a short stop in El Salvador.
